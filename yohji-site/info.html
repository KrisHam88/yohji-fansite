More than a designer, Yohji Yamamoto represents a philosophy. His work invites people to see fashion not simply as decoration, but as a way of thinking, questioning, and expressing individuality.


1943 — Born in Tokyo, Japan

1969 — Bunka Fashion College

1972 — Launches Y’s

1977 — First Tokyo show

1981 — Paris debut

1984 — Mainichi Award

1985 — Chevalier, Ordre des Arts et des Lettres

1990 — Officer, Ordre des Arts et des Lettres

2002 — Y-3 with Adidas

2009 — Bankruptcy

2010 — Comeback

2011 — Commander, Ordre des Arts et des Lettres

2016 — CFDA Award

2021 — 40 years in Paris

Present — Active career
